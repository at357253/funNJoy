Introduction To Our School

Fun 'n' Joy is an all-inclusive daycare and preschool which focuses on the all round development of your child. We, at Fun 'n' Joy help identify and nurture the potential your child has, and encourage the development of the child's skills.


Facilities Fun n Joy Preschool Creche Integrated playschool and creche Fully Air-conditioned Open 8 AM - 8 PM Under CCTV Surveillance Indoor play area Optional Pickup / Drop off facilities Trained teachers and caregivers Help with Home Task Nutritious meals Optional Activities like Personality Development, Art & Craft, Dance etc.

About Us

Your child is unique. Which is why we give individualized attention to each child to support their developmental milestones which are critical in the early years.



Our method of education is designed to foster a child's natural learning process. The children are encouraged to think on their own, express their ideas and feelings, make decisions, and develop their own solutions.
